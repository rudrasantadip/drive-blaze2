Problem Statements

1. Disaster Management System

Story: In 2023, a coastal town was hit by a massive cyclone that disrupted communication, electricity, and emergency services. Many lives were lost because people couldn’t get real-time information or request help efficiently.

Problem Statement: Design a disaster management system that can operate in low-connectivity areas, providing real-time updates, emergency alerts, and a platform for people to request assistance. The system should also allow for coordination among emergency services, volunteers, and the local government.

2. Personalized Healthcare Assistant

Story: An elderly man living alone was diagnosed with multiple chronic conditions, including diabetes and hypertension. Managing his medications, diet, and doctor's appointments became overwhelming, leading to frequent hospital visits.

Problem Statement: Develop a personalized healthcare assistant application that helps patients manage their chronic conditions. The app should include features like medication reminders, diet suggestions based on health conditions, and appointment scheduling. Additionally, it should integrate with wearable devices to monitor vital signs in real-time and alert caregivers or doctors if something is wrong.

3. Smart Education Platform

Story: During the COVID-19 pandemic, a rural school struggled to continue education online due to a lack of resources and internet connectivity. Many students fell behind because they couldn’t access the materials or interact with their teachers.

Problem Statement: Create a smart education platform that is accessible in low-bandwidth environments and provides interactive, personalized learning experiences. The platform should include offline content access, real-time teacher-student interaction, and tools for tracking student progress. It should also support multiple languages and be adaptable for different age groups.

4. Sustainable Urban Mobility

Story: A city known for its high traffic congestion is facing increasing pollution levels and a decline in the quality of life for its residents. Commuters spend hours in traffic daily, leading to frustration and economic loss.

Problem Statement: Design a sustainable urban mobility solution that reduces traffic congestion and pollution in a metropolitan city. The solution could include a combination of public transportation enhancements, ride-sharing, electric vehicle incentives, and smart traffic management systems. The goal is to make commuting more efficient, affordable, and environmentally friendly.

5. AI-Powered Mental Health Support

Story: A university student struggling with anxiety and depression finds it difficult to seek help due to the stigma surrounding mental health. As a result, their academic performance and personal life suffer.

Problem Statement: Develop an AI-powered mental health support system that provides students with confidential, round-the-clock assistance. The system should include features like mood tracking, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) exercises, and access to professional counselors through chat or video calls. It should also provide users with resources to help them manage stress, anxiety, and depression in a safe and supportive environment.

6. Open Innovation

Participants are encouraged to bring forward any innovative idea or solution they are passionate about. The goal is to think outside the box and address a problem of your choice using technology
