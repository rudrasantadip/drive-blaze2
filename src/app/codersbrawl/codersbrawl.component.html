Algo Arcade

September 28th, 2024 | 10:00 AM - 02:00 PM

Welcome to Algo Arcade, an intense online coding contest where the sharpest minds battle it out to solve complex problems. Prepare yourself for an exhilarating challenge that will test your programming skills and problem-solving abilities to the limit!

Event Timeline

  • 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Introduction & Briefing
  • 10:30 AM - 01:30 PM: Coding Round
  • 01:30 PM - 02:00 PM: Submission & Results

Guidelines

  • Participants will be given a set of coding problems to solve within the time limit.
  • Use of any programming language is allowed.
  • Solutions will be judged based on correctness, efficiency, and coding style.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.
  • The top performers will be awarded prizes based on their performance.
