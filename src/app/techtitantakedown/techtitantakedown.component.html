Titan Takedown

September 28th, 2024 | 02:00 PM - 05:00 PM

Welcome to Titan Takedown, the ultimate showdown in the world of cybersecurity! Prepare yourself for an intense battle between two teams: Attackers and Defenders. The Attackers will unleash their skills to breach the security of a website, while the Defenders will use their knowledge to fortify and protect it.

Event Timeline

  • 02:00 PM - 02:30 PM: Introduction & Briefing
  • 02:30 PM - 03:00 PM: Team Formation & Strategy
  • 03:00 PM - 04:30 PM: Attack & Defense Round
  • 04:30 PM - 05:00 PM: Debriefing & Results

Guidelines

  • Each team will have 30 minutes to strategize before the attack begins.
  • Attackers are allowed to use any tools or techniques at their disposal.
  • Defenders must work within the given infrastructure to protect the website.
  • The use of any malicious code or software that causes irreversible damage is prohibited.
  • The team that successfully breaches or defends the website within the given time wins.
