Tech for Eye

September 29th, 2024 | 03:00 PM - 06:00 PM

Welcome to Tech for Eye, a technical photography contest that challenges you to capture the essence of technology through your lens. Unleash your creativity and showcase your technical skills in this unique event!

Event Timeline

  • 03:00 PM - 03:30 PM: Introduction & Briefing
  • 03:30 PM - 05:30 PM: Photography Session
  • 05:30 PM - 06:00 PM: Submission & Judging

Guidelines

  • Participants must capture photographs that align with the theme of technology.
  • Each participant is allowed to submit up to 3 photographs.
  • Photos can be edited but must retain their original essence.
  • Plagiarism or use of others' work will result in disqualification.
  • The top entries will be awarded based on creativity, technical execution, and adherence to the theme.
